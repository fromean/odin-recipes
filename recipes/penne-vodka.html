Penne Vodka

A Delicious Penne Vodka Recipe Straight From Italy

Imagine a day roaming the streets of Rome. You come home and find yourself starving, but you spent your last Euros on a bottle of limoncello. You open your cabinets and find you have all the ingredients needed to make a delicious penne vodka. The combination of flavor and carbs will give you the energy you need to have just as much fun tomorrow!

Ingredients

  • Shallots
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Vodka
  • Penne Pasta

Steps

  1. Melt butter over medium heat. Add in shallots and garlic, cook until soft and golden brown
  2. Stir in tomato paste and crushed tomatoes. Let simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Add Vodka, cook over medium high heat until bubbling
  4. Lower to low heat, stir in heavy cream and let simmer
  5. Add in cooked and drained pasta, and stir
