Sauteed Spinach

Some leafy greens to satisfy Mother Nature

We simply could not leave you with a full meal minus vegetables. That's why we're providing the ultimate green to pair with your Italian feast: sauteed spinach. Not only will it satisfy your taste buds, but it will help you reach that goal of 5 healthy foods per day!

Ingredients

  • Baby spinach
  • Garlic, minced
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add minced garlic. Sautee until golden brown
  2. Add in spinach and stir until cooked
  3. Spice up your spinach by adding crushed red pepper, garlic powder, and salt to taste
  4. Stir until fully mixed, remove from heat when done
