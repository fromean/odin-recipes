Chicken Parmesan

A beautiful cutlet of chicken parmesan to go with your penne vodka

Surely after working up a sweat strolling the streets of Rome all day, penne vodka itself won't be enough. Let's add some protein to your meal with a delicious cutlet of chicken parmesan. The hearty Italian staple will full up your tummy and satisfy your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • Egg
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Frying oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs in a bowl. Dip chicken breast into egg mixture, then into bread crumbs
  2. Place breaded cutlet into hot frying oil. Cook for two minutes on both sides, or until golden brown
  3. Place finished cutlets on paper towel to dry excess oil off. Heat oven to .400 while you wait
  4. Once all cutlets are done, place on baking sheet. Cover with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese
  5. Bake for 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted
